FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

1. How long does it take to make a website?

A. It depends on your specific requirements. A small-business website with up to 5 pages and basic features can be developed in a couple of business days.

2. Do you provide hosting and domain registration services?

A. While I don't provide hosting and domain registration services directly, I can recommend reputable hosting providers and assist you with setting up your domain and hosting. If you're a small business not expecting a huge amount of traffic, I can suggest free hosting solutions.

3. Do you help with maintenance and updates after the project is completed?

A. Yes, I offer yearly website maintenance services to ensure your website remains up-to-date with the latest security patches and feature enhancements.

4. Will my website be mobile-friendly?

A. Absolutely! I can make websites optimized for viewing on various devices, including smartphones, tablets, and desktops.

5. How do you ensure my website is search engine friendly (SEO)?

A. I follow SEO best practices during the development process, which including optimizing the website structure, meta tags, images, and content. Please note: Don't expect miracles. Google has a "sandbox"/probation window before it ranks brand-new websites, which may last 6 months or even longer. The web development needs to play into a full-fledged SEO strategy.

6. Can you integrate third-party services such as payment gateways and social media platforms?

A. Yes, I have experience integrating various third-party services and APIs, including payment gateways like PayPal and Stripe, as well as social media platforms such as Facebook, Twitter, and Instagram.

7. Can you set up website analytics and tracking for me?

A. Yes, I can set up popular analytics tools like Google Analytics to track visitor behavior, monitor website performance, and gather valuable insights.

8. Do you offer content creation services or do I need to provide the content for my website?

A. I offer content creation services at a negotiable per-word rate (writing copy for a basic website can take up to a week). Alternatively, you can provide your own content and I'll ensure it's optimized for the web.

9. How much does a website cost?

A. I charge a competitive local rate to a) make your site, b) deploy your site, and c) maintain it for a year.
Please note: Any post-deployment structural or cosmetic changes cost extra. Content creation is not included as part of the package.

10. Do you also make mobile apps?

A. Yes, I make mobile apps. Note: Publishing to Google Play or the iOS store isn't guaranteed but you can host the app on your website.
